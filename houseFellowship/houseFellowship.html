What Is It

More Than a Meeting

House Fellowship is the heartbeat of Winner Chapel. These are intimate gatherings held in the homes of members throughout Omaha where believers come together to pray, study the Word, and support one another in real life.

Whether you're new to the faith or have walked with God for years, there is a group for you. Find a group near you and take your next step.

Bible Study
Deep dive into the Word of God in a warm, welcoming setting.
Prayer Together
Intercede for one another, your community, and the nations.
Real Community
Build genuine relationships with fellow believers.
Spiritual Growth
Be discipled and grow in your walk with God week by week.
