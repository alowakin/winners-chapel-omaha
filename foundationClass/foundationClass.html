New to the Faith?

Foundation Class

A 6-week discipleship program designed to ground every new believer in the foundational truths of the Christian faith.

Home  /  Foundation Class

Build Your Life on the Word

Whether you just gave your life to Christ or you've been in church for years and want a solid foundation, this class is for you. Over 6 weeks, you will discover who you are in Christ, how to pray, how to study the Bible, and how to live victoriously as a believer. All materials are provided — completely free of charge.

Curriculum

What You'll Learn

01
Salvation & New Birth
Understanding what it means to be born again and the assurance of your salvation in Christ.
02
The Word of God
How to read, study, and apply the Bible to every area of your life for lasting transformation.
03
Prayer & Communication
Building a powerful prayer life — learning the different types of prayer and how to hear God's voice.
04
The Holy Spirit
Who is the Holy Spirit, His role in your life, and how to walk in His power every day.
05
The Local Church
Why the church matters, how to serve, give, and grow within the body of Christ.
06
Living the Faith
How to apply your faith in daily life — at work, in relationships, in finances, and in your calling.
