New to the Faith?

Foundation Class

A 6-week discipleship program designed to ground every new believer in the foundational truths of the Christian faith.

Home  /  Foundation Class

Build Your Life on the Word

Every building that will last requires a foundation — so does every believer who will mature and abide in the Kingdom. The Believers Foundation School is a 6-lesson discipleship program built entirely on Scripture. The Bible is not a historical account of God's dealings with man; it is God's manual for victorious and fulfilled living. Whether you just gave your life to Christ or have been in church for years, this class will establish you in the truths that produce lasting results. All materials are provided completely free of charge.

Curriculum

What You'll Learn

01
The Word of God
The Bible is God's manual for victorious and fulfilled living. Discover what the Word of God is, why every believer must study it, and how to read, meditate on, and apply it daily.
02
Faith — The Faith That Works
Faith is a display of confidence in God and His Word until the desired result is obtained. Learn what faith is, why you need it, and how to activate it through declaration and bold steps of action.
03
Prevailing Prayers
Prayer is two-way communication with God (Jer 33:3). Understand why every believer must pray, how to pray effectively in Jesus' name, and the forces that supercharge a prayer life.
04
The Holy Spirit
The Holy Spirit is a Person — the third member of the Godhead and your Comforter, Counselor, and Guide. Learn who He is, why every believer must be baptized with the Holy Spirit, and how to receive Him.
05
Covenant of Financial Prosperity
It is God's will for believers to prosper (3 John 1:2). Discover the scriptural basis for financial prosperity and the covenant obligations — tithing, worship offering, and giving — that activate God's blessing.
06
Kingdom Service
You were created for good works (Eph 2:10). Explore how to use your talents, time, and resources to serve God's kingdom through prayer, evangelism, and giving to advance the gospel.
