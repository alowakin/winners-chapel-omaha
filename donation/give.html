Questions

Frequently Asked

Is my donation tax-deductible? ▼
Yes. Winner Chapel is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. You will receive a giving statement at the end of the year.
How do I know my gift was received? ▼
Each payment platform (Cash App, Zelle, PayPal) sends you an instant confirmation. For questions, contact us at giving@winnerschapel.org.
Can I give in person? ▼
Absolutely. Offering envelopes are available during every Sunday service. You are welcome to give cash or check during the offertory.
What if I have questions about my giving? ▼
Reach out to our finance team at giving@winnerschapel.org or speak with any of our ushers after service. We are happy to help.
