Who We Are

Our Story

Winner Chapel at Omaha is a local assembly of Living Faith Church Worldwide — a global ministry founded by Bishop David Oyedepo, one of Africa's most influential Christian leaders. Our mandate flows directly from an 18-hour vision received from the Lord: to liberate mankind in all facets of human existence.

We are a Spirit-filled, Word-based church committed to preaching the undiluted gospel of Jesus Christ. From our home in Omaha, Nebraska, we reach into our community and across the nations with the message of faith, healing, and restoration.

Every service is a supernatural encounter — whether you come broken, searching, or ready to grow, you will leave transformed by the power of God's Word.

"The hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil, through the preaching of the word of faith." Luke 4:18–19  |  Matthew 10:1–8
What We Believe

Our Core Values

01
The Word of God
We are a Word-based church. Every sermon, every service, every decision flows from the infallible Word of God. We believe the Bible is the final authority in all matters of life and faith.
02
Prayer & Worship
We are a house of prayer. From the Covenant Hour of Prayer at 5:30 AM to our Sunday Upper Room Prayer, intercession is the backbone of everything we do.
03
Faith That Works
We believe in a living, active faith. Not just a mental agreement with scripture, but a faith that produces miracles, healing, and deliverance in daily life.
04
Community & Fellowship
We are stronger together. Through our House Fellowship groups and church family, we build lasting relationships rooted in love and mutual accountability.
05
Generosity & Giving
We honor God with our finances. Tithing, offering, and sowing into missions are acts of worship that release blessing and advance the Kingdom of God.
06
Reaching the Nations
Omaha is our home but the world is our mission field. We send, support, and pray for gospel work across every nation — until every knee bows.
